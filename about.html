Our story

About Belarro

New plants for professional kitchens. Grown to order. Delivered weekly.

Belarro grows microgreens and specialty plants for professional kitchens in Berlin. We focus on plants you won't find through standard distribution channels.

How we grow

Everything is grown on real soil, not hydroponic sponge. This produces better flavor, better texture, and plants that perform in real kitchen environments. We seed after you order — nothing sits around waiting.

How we deliver

Weekly delivery on a consistent rhythm. Same day, same time, same quantities. You set it once and it runs by default. Change only when your menu changes or when seasons shift.

Why Berlin

Short distances matter for delicate plants. Farm to kitchen in hours, not days. This means longer shelf life, better freshness, and plants that actually work in service.

Who we serve

Professional kitchens that want specialty plants without the operational complexity. Restaurants, hotels, caterers, and private chefs who value consistency and quality.

Our principles

What we believe

  • Consistency over complexity
  • Real soil, real flavor
  • Grown to order, never stockpiled
  • Short distances, longer freshness
  • Service first, innovation when it fits
