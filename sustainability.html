Sustainability

How Belarro approaches resource use.

  • Local growing — All plants are grown in Berlin, reducing transport distance.
  • Grow-to-order — We seed after you order. No speculative production. No stock that goes to waste.
  • Reduced waste — Weekly rhythm means consistent quantities. Less overproduction, less disposal.
  • Reduced transport — Short distances from farm to kitchen. Delivered directly.
  • Soil-based growing — Plants are grown on soil, not hydroponic sponges.
